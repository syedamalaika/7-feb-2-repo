About GTTI College

Government Technical Training Institute (GTTI) is a premier institution dedicated to providing world-class technical education and vocational training. Established with a vision to create skilled professionals, we have been at the forefront of technical education for over two decades.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry-aligned curriculum ensure that our students are well-prepared to meet the challenges of the modern workforce.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Matriculation (10th Grade) with minimum 50% marks
  • Age between 15-25 years
  • Valid CNIC or B-Form
  • Medical fitness certificate

Required Documents

  • Matriculation Certificate & Mark Sheet
  • CNIC Copy (Student & Guardian)
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Recent Passport Size Photographs (4 copies)
  • Character Certificate
  • Medical Certificate
